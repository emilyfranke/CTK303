BLM Campsites Hikes Route

The Route

Take I-70 to Colorado and then stay one night in Colorado. Driving the rest of the way to camp in Canyonlands/Arches for three nights. Drive the 5 hours on 89 to Bryce Canyon to stop for 2 more nights. Drive back stop in Nebraska for one night and then head back to Illinois! Click on "Route" for a map.

Hikes

Looking at "Green River Overlook" for an easy hike in Canyonlands and then classics like "The Delicate Arch" in Arches National Park. "Joint Trail", "Peek-a-boo Canyon" and "Spooky Gulch" are slot canyons in the backcountry. Click on "Hikes" for All Trails information.
